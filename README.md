## üìë Excel Module  

- **Data Import & Cleaning**  
  The raw Zomato dataset is imported into Excel where duplicates are removed, missing values are handled, and column formats are standardized. This step ensures the dataset is reliable and ready for deeper analysis.  

- **Data Preparation**  
  Calculated fields such as revenue, average order value, and customer frequency are created. Cuisines are categorized, and text entries are normalized to maintain consistency across the dataset.  

- **Exploratory Analysis**  
  Pivot tables and descriptive statistics are used to explore customer demographics, restaurant ratings, and order volumes. This helps identify early patterns and anomalies in the data.  

- **KPI Calculation**  
  Key metrics such as total orders, average ratings, top cuisines, and monthly revenue growth are derived. These KPIs form the foundation for performance tracking.  

- **Trend Analysis**  
  Seasonal order patterns, peak ordering times, and customer activity changes are identified. This helps businesses understand demand cycles and plan accordingly.  

- **Visualization**  
  Basic charts (bar, line, pie) are created to present insights clearly. These visuals serve as a quick overview before moving into BI tools for advanced dashboards.
